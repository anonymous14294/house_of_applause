The Core Engine: Composite Scoring Logic

How we measure and drive behavior on the Dedicated Leaderboards.

To ensure a balanced approach that values quality without sacrificing efficiency, we utilize a Weighted Composite Score (out of 100). This single metric powers both the Monthly Leaderboard and Quarterly Maestro awards.
