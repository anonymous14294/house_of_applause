The Monthly & Quarterly Process

To ensure fairness and transparency, our automated awards follow a clear 5-day process at the start of each new period.

Day 1

Data Extraction

Operations pulls all raw KPI data for the previous period.

Day 2

Calculation

Composite scores are calculated and leaderboards are generated.

Day 3

Verification

Managers vet the top candidates for qualification and quality.

Day 4

Final Approval

The Support Manager signs off on the final list of winners.

Day 5

Announcement!

Winners are announced, and rewards are processed.
